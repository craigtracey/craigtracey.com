Craig Tracey

Craig is currently the Automation Technical Lead at HubSpot. Day-to-day he drives a small team of engineers delivering infrasctructure automation solutions that manage many thousands instances across various cloud providers; including AWS, Rackspace, and OpenStack.

Before HubSpot, Craig helped build scaleable backup solutions at Carbonite, delivered vmkernel network stack changes at VMWare, and helped address system-level defects in early releases of the Netezza data warehouse appliance.

As he believes that OpenStack is poised to democratize cloud computing, he has focused most of his recent open source efforts towards contributing to various projects within that ecosystem. Some of this work may be found on GitHub, Launchpad and OpenStack Code Review

When not hacking on code, he can be found running, playing ice hockey, or out with friends in his hometown of Boston, Massachusetts.
